Getting Started

OneVictoria is the official resident engagement platform for the Municipality of Victoria. This digital hub connects citizens with municipal services, community events, volunteer programs, and civic announcements. The platform enables residents to:

  • Register for community events and programs
  • Submit volunteer applications for municipal initiatives
  • Track community service hour contributions
  • Make monetary or in-kind donations to support local causes
  • Receive real-time notifications regarding community updates
  • Manage personal profile information securely
  1. Navigate to the OneVictoria portal homepage
  2. On the authentication panel, select "Create Account"
  3. Complete the registration form with your full name, email, phone, age, gender, and address
  4. Create a secure password (minimum 6 characters)
  5. Confirm your password and click "Create Account"
Tip: Use an active email address as this will serve as your primary credential for account recovery and notifications.
  1. Visit the OneVictoria portal
  2. Enter your registered email address and password
  3. Select "Access Portal"
Important: If you cannot access your account, please refer to the Troubleshooting section or contact support.

Account Management

  1. Click your profile picture in the header to open Profile Settings
  2. Select "Edit Profile" to enable editing mode
  3. Modify editable fields: Mobile Number, Address, and Profile Picture
  4. Select "Save Changes" to apply updates
Note: Name, email, gender, and age are fixed fields. Contact support to request changes to these.
  1. Go to Profile Settings
  2. Click "Edit Profile"
  3. Enter your new password in the password field
  4. Save your changes

Events & Registration

  1. Go to News & Events > Events from the header dropdown
  2. Browse available upcoming events
  3. Click "Register / Join" on any event card
  4. Confirm your registration in the dialog
  1. Go to My Events
  2. Click "Cancel Registration" on the event card
  3. Confirm the cancellation
Tip: Cancel at least 24 hours before the event to allow others to participate.
Registered Your registration has been processed successfully.
Completed You attended the event and received service hour credit.
Cancelled Registration was cancelled by you or the organizer.

Volunteering

  1. Go to Community > Volunteer
  2. Select your primary skill and experience level
  3. Upload skill verification document (certificate or proof)
  4. Choose your availability
  5. Click "Submit Application"
Applications undergo review by organizers. You'll receive a notification upon status change.

Accepted formats (max 5MB):

  • PDF — Certificates, training documents, licenses
  • JPG / PNG — Photos of certificates or credentials
  • DOC / DOCX — Reference letters, verification documents

Service Hours

Service hours are accrued through active participation in registered community events. When you attend an event, the organizer may certify your participation hours. These are recorded in your Service Hours tab.

Approved Hours verified by an authorized organizer.
Pending Hours awaiting verification from the organizer.

Donations

  1. Go to Community > Donations
  2. Enter the item or amount and purpose
  3. Click "Submit Donation"
  4. Select payment method (GCash, PayMaya, Bank Transfer, or Cash)
  5. Enter amount and your name
  6. Click "Pay Now"
  • GCash — Mobile wallet payment
  • PayMaya — Digital wallet transaction
  • Bank Transfer — Direct deposit
  • Cash — In-person at designated offices

Notifications

  • Quick Preview: Click the bell icon in the header for recent notifications
  • Full View: Click "See All Notifications" to view the complete list
  • Click "Mark All Read" in the notification panel
  • Click on individual notifications to view and auto-mark as read

Troubleshooting

Contact our support team using the methods in Contact Support. Provide your registered email for identity verification, and a representative will guide you through the password reset process.

  • Refresh your browser (F5 or Ctrl+R)
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
  • Verify internet connection stability
  • Ensure JavaScript is enabled

Applications undergo manual review (1-3 business days). You'll receive a notification when your status changes. If more than 3 days have passed, contact support.
