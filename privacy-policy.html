Privacy Policy

OneVictoria Resident Hub

Effective: January 1, 2026 Last Updated: July 22, 2026
1

Introduction & Scope

Your privacy matters to us

Welcome to OneVictoria, the official digital Resident Hub of the Municipality of Victoria, Tarlac. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy rights.

This Privacy Policy explains in clear, transparent terms how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our platform — including account registration, event participation, volunteer applications, and donation transactions.

Legal Basis: We comply with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines, its implementing rules and regulations, and all relevant local ordinances of Victoria, Tarlac.

By accessing or using OneVictoria, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2

Information We Collect

Transparent data collection

We collect only information that is necessary to provide and improve our municipal services. Below are the categories of data we may process:

Personal Identification

Full name, email address, mobile number, age, gender, and residential address provided during account creation or profile updates.

Service-Related Data

Volunteer skills, availability, uploaded documents, service hours records, and event registration details.

Donation Records

Donation amount, purpose, payment method reference (GCash, PayMaya, bank transfer), and donor identification.

Technical & Usage Data

IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, and platform interaction logs for security and analytics.

3

How We Use Your Information

Purpose-driven processing

Your data is processed strictly for legitimate municipal purposes and to enhance community services:

  • Account Management — Create and manage your resident account, verify your identity, and provide secure access to the Resident Hub.
  • Community Services — Process event registrations, volunteer applications, and accurately track community service hours.
  • Donation Processing — Facilitate donations, generate official receipts, and maintain transparent public donation logs (donor names displayed only with explicit consent).
  • Important Notifications — Send critical updates regarding your activities, municipal announcements, emergency alerts, and policy changes.
  • Platform Improvement — Debug technical issues, analyze aggregated usage trends, and enhance platform security for better municipal planning.
  • Legal Compliance — Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from authorized government authorities.
4

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for commercial purposes. Data sharing is limited to the following circumstances:

  • Municipal Departments — Authorized personnel from the Mayor's Office, Social Welfare, Disaster Risk Reduction, and other relevant local government units to coordinate community programs effectively.
  • Service Providers — Trusted third-party vendors (e.g., cloud hosting, payment gateways) who are contractually bound to protect your data and use it solely for designated municipal services.
  • Legal Compliance — When required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of the Municipality of Victoria, its residents, or the public.
  • Public Acknowledgment — With your explicit consent, your name and donation details may appear in public gratitude lists. Otherwise, all donations remain anonymous by default.
5

Data Security Measures

Protecting what matters

We implement robust administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security framework includes:

Encryption

SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit

Secure Infrastructure

Protected servers with access controls

Regular Audits

Continuous security monitoring & updates

Important: Despite our best efforts, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We continuously update our practices to align with industry standards and government regulations.

6

Data Retention Policy

Keeping data only as needed

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by Philippine law.

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your resident profile remains active on the platform.
  • Account Deactivation: Upon your request for account deactivation or data deletion, we will securely dispose of your data within a reasonable timeframe (typically 30-60 days).
  • Legal Requirements: Certain records may be retained for extended periods when mandated for legal, auditing, tax, or archival public interest purposes as required by Philippine law.
7

Your Data Privacy Rights

You are in control

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you are entitled to the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to be Informed

Know how your personal data is being processed.

Right to Access

Request a copy of your personal data we hold.

Right to Rectification

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.

Right to Object

Withdraw consent for specific uses of your data.

Right to Erasure or Blocking

Request deletion of your data under certain conditions.

Right to File a Complaint

Lodge a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer using the details in Section 9. We will respond to your request within the period prescribed by law.

8

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Minimal & essential only

OneVictoria uses essential cookies and similar technologies to maintain session integrity, remember your preferences, and enhance your user experience.

What we DON'T do:

  • • We do not use third-party advertising cookies
  • • We do not track you across other websites
  • • We do not sell your browsing data

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the Resident Hub platform.

Changes to This Policy

Staying current

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will:

  • Notify all registered residents through the OneVictoria notification system
  • Post the revised version with a new effective date prominently on the platform
  • Provide a summary of material changes where applicable

Your continued use of the OneVictoria platform after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices.
